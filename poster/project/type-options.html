Cold Diffusion

Inverting arbitrary image transforms — without noise.

1 — Motivation

Does diffusion really need noise?

Standard diffusion is theorized via Langevin dynamics and variational inference, both of which assume Gaussian noise. We re-implement the deblurring pipeline of Bansal et al. to test whether the noise is load-bearing.
